Student - it's a word which brings to mind a picture of a child dressed spick and span in a neatly pressed uniform, shouldering a bag which seems to have three times the size of the child's body, wearing a tie up to the neck, belt around the waist, whose features on the face are moulded into an expression of eagerness, innocence, timidness and slyness..all bundled into one solid expression- the expression of a student. But maybe that's only in the good old days, when we were kids without a care in the world, only half aware of the burdens and privileges we amy be granted in the near future, a child with a hearty and soulful laugh, eyes which are alert and prying.

Now, student - it's a word which brings to mind a picture of someone who is dressed in the most casual wear you can think of - straight from bed in tracks and tees, hair barely combed, ruffled and unruly, lucky  to find a pen in the pocket to play with during a particularly boring lecture ;) . The features of the face are moulded into an expression of eternel sleepiness, eyes drooping, stiffling a yawn, barely awake, with the thought that the prof. shouldn't notice this clumsy wreck who aparently had the nerve to show up in class, only to get attendance.
